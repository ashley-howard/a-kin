I aim to experiment with any work that I do, both compositionally and visually. Too not do so is to walk in circles around someone else's footsteps.

Conventional music has been stagnant for a while and this has been aided by an industry built on making money and not on making art. It's always been arts task to push at what the human experience is. I am not talking about simply asking questions about the human experience but more to push at how we experience things. Take painting for an example; you use your imagination and the tools at hand to create a world that is completely separate from our own, so why would you then fill that world with picture perfect copies of what exists in our own world when creativity is endless.
